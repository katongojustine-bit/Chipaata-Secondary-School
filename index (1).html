Chipaata Day Secondary School is a co-educational institution serving the community of Chitambo District in Central Province. The school maintains a focused learning environment, offers equal opportunities to all learners, and is committed to academic growth and responsible conduct.

About Us

School Background

Established to serve the educational needs of Chitambo District, Chipaata Day Secondary School has been a pillar of academic excellence since its founding. Our institution has grown to become a model for community-based education in Central Province.

Co-Education Approach

We believe in providing equal opportunities for all students. Our co-educational environment fosters mutual respect, collaboration, and prepares students for diverse real-world interactions. Both genders participate equally in all academic and extracurricular activities.

Leadership

Under the guidance of Principal Justine Katongo, our leadership team is committed to maintaining high educational standards while nurturing responsible citizenship among our students.

Community Role

We actively engage with the Chitambo community through outreach programs, adult education initiatives, and cultural preservation projects. The school serves as a community hub for educational and social development.

Admissions

Enrollment Guidelines

Admission is open to all qualifying students from Chitambo District and surrounding areas. Priority is given to students from feeder primary schools in our catchment area.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Grade 7 school leaving certificate
  • Birth certificate
  • Two passport-sized photographs
  • Medical report from recognized clinic

Important Dates

  • Application period: January 5-30 annually
  • Entrance tests: First week of February
  • Admission letters: March 1
  • Orientation: April 10
